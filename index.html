About

MMSO, derived from the names Marzoug, Mahfoud, Sidqui, and Ouafik, represents a group of digital development interns. Founded by its owners, MMSO serves as a platform for interns to collaborate on web development projects, fostering teamwork and skill-sharing among its members. Through MMSO, interns engage in collective learning experiences and leverage each other's expertise to enhance their digital proficiency.

Objectives

At MMSO, our objectives are straightforward: to master Git for version control, delve into essential web development languages like JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, and explore frameworks such as Bootstrap, Tailwind CSS, and W3.CSS. We prioritize teamwork, aiming to emulate the collaborative spirit seen on platforms like GitHub. By focusing on these objectives, we equip ourselves with the skills needed to excel in digital development projects.
